Defining GIScience & GIS

GIS

To me, GIS is the use of software and hardware to capture, process, and display spatial data. It allows us to analyze data and present it in a meaningful and comprehensible way.

GIScience

To me, GIScience is the academic discipline that includes GIS. It looks at data but also at the ways to present it, the various methods to use, and the ethical questions surrounding data presentation.
